Tented is an AI-native go-to-market platform that empowers you to create personalized, real-time landing pages. With Tented, you can generate beautiful, modern web pages using natural language prompts and then iterate on them through an intuitive chat interface.

## What is a Tent?

A **Tent** is your personalized landing page - a complete, standalone HTML page that can be customized for different audiences, campaigns, or use cases. Each tent includes:

- **Modern, responsive design** with full-width layouts
- **Interactive elements** and animations
- **Form handling** with built-in submission processing
- **SEO optimization** and proper meta tags
- **Brand customization** options

## Get Started in Three Steps

Follow this guide to create your first tent, make edits, and publish it to the web.

### Step 1: Create Your First Tent

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="plus" title="Start with a prompt">
    Creating a tent is as simple as describing what you want in natural language.
    
    1. **Sign in** to your Tented account at [app.tented.ai](https://app.tented.ai)
    2. **Click "Create New Tent"** on the dashboard
    3. **Write your prompt** describing your landing page:
       - "Create a landing page for a SaaS productivity tool with a hero section, features, and pricing"
       - "Build a landing page for a coffee shop with menu, location, and contact form"
       - "Design a portfolio page for a freelance designer with projects and testimonials"
    4. **Click "Generate Tent"** and watch as AI creates your page
    
    <Tip>Be specific about your industry, target audience, and key features you want to highlight!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="image" title="Add images and branding">
    Enhance your tent with visual elements:
    
    - **Upload images** to include logos, product photos, or graphics
    - **Set brand colors** to match your company's visual identity
    - **Choose fonts** that align with your brand personality
    - **Add your company name** and tagline
    
    <Tip>Images help AI understand your brand better and create more relevant designs!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Edit and Iterate

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="comments" title="Chat with your tent">
    Once your tent is generated, you can refine it through natural conversation:
    
    - **"Make the hero section more prominent"**
    - **"Change the color scheme to blue and white"**
    - **"Add a testimonials section"**
    - **"Make the call-to-action button larger"**
    - **"Add a contact form at the bottom"**
    
    The AI will make these changes in real-time while you watch the preview update.
    
    <Tip>You can see both the live preview and the code as you make changes!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="code" title="View and edit code">
    For advanced users, you can also:
    
    - **View the generated HTML/CSS/JavaScript code**
    - **Make direct code edits** if needed
    - **Copy the code** for external use
    - **Download the complete file**
    
    <Tip>The code is clean, modern, and production-ready!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Publish to the Web

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Publish your tent">
    Make your tent live on the web:
    
    1. **Click "Publish"** in the tent editor
    2. **Choose your domain** (or use the default tented.ai subdomain)
    3. **Get your live URL** - your tent is now accessible worldwide!
    4. **Share the link** with your audience
    
    <Tip>Your tent gets a unique URL that you can share, embed, or use in campaigns!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Track form submissions">
    Monitor engagement with built-in analytics:
    
    - **View form submissions** in real-time
    - **Export contact data** for follow-up
    - **Track conversion rates** and engagement
    - **Set up notifications** for new submissions
    
    <Tip>All form submissions are automatically processed and stored securely!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Prompts

- **Be specific** about your industry and target audience
- **Mention key features** you want to highlight
- **Include your brand personality** (professional, playful, modern, etc.)
- **Specify the page purpose** (lead generation, product showcase, contact, etc.)

### Iterating Effectively

- **Start broad** with major layout changes
- **Get specific** with colors, fonts, and styling
- **Test on mobile** by checking the responsive preview
- **Focus on conversion** by optimizing call-to-action elements

### Publishing Successfully

- **Test your tent** before publishing to ensure everything works
- **Check form functionality** by submitting test data
- **Verify mobile responsiveness** across different devices
- **Share strategically** with your target audience
