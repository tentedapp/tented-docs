## What is a Tent?

A **Tent** is your personalized landing page - a complete, standalone HTML page you can customize for different audiences, campaigns, or use cases. Each tent includes:

- **Modern, responsive design** with full-width layouts
- **Interactive elements** and animations
- **Form handling** with built-in submission processing
- **SEO optimization** and proper meta tags
- **Brand customization** options

## Get Started in Four Steps

Here's a high-level overview of the process to create, edit, and publish your first tent.

### Step 1: Add Your Branding

    Upload your branding assets to match your company's visual identity:
    
    - **Upload your logo** to appear in the header.
    - **Set your brand colors** for buttons and accents.
    - **Upload a company icon** for browser tabs.

    ![A screenshot of the dialog box for adding a **Primary Brand Color**, **Brand Logo**, and **Brand Icon**.](../images/add-branding.png)

### Step 2: Create Your First Tent

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pencil" title="Write your prompt">
    Creating a tent is as simple as describing what you want in natural language.
    
       - "Create a landing page for a SaaS productivity tool with a hero section, features, and pricing"
       - "Design a portfolio page for a freelance designer with projects and testimonials"
    
      ![A screenshot of a detailed prompt for an early-access signup page for a new feature called "Predictive Scheduling". The prompt is "Generate a landing page for our new feature, 'Predictive Scheduling'. The page must be high-converting and focused solely on capturing early access signups. Include a hero section, features, and pricing. Use a professional tone."](../images/initial-prompt.png)

    For more information on writing effective prompts, see our [Best Practices for AI Prompting](../working-with-tents/best-practices-prompting) guide.

      ![A screenshot of the prompt and the resulting tent preview.](../images/initial-prompt-response.png)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="image" title="Add images and personalization">
    Enhance your tent with visuals and more:
    
    - **Upload images** to include product photos or graphics.
    - **Add downloadable files** like brochures or white papers.
    
    ![A screenshot of a prompt to upload and insert an image with the final result.](../images/upload-image-via-prompt.png)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Edit and Iterate

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="comments" title="Chat with your tent">
    Once your tent is generated, you can refine it through natural conversation:
    
    - "Change the color scheme to blue and white"
    - "Add a testimonials section"
    - "Make the call-to-action button larger"
    - "Add a contact form at the bottom"
    
    The AI will make these changes in real-time while you watch the preview update.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="code" title="View and edit code">
    For advanced users, you can also:
    
    - View the generated HTML/CSS/JavaScript code.
    - Make direct code edits if needed.
    - Download the complete file.
    
    ![A screenshot of the code view with the code icon highlighted.](../images/code-mode.png)

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Publish to the Web

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Publish your tent">
    Make your tent live on the web:
    
    1. Click **Publish** in the tent editor.
    2. Confirm publishing in the dialog box.
    3. **Copy your live URL**. Your tent is now accessible worldwide!
    4. **Share the link** with your audience.
    
    ![A screenshot the publishing flow.](../images/publish.png)    
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Track form submissions">
    Monitor engagement with built-in analytics:
    
    - **View form submissions** in real-time.
    - **Export contact data** for follow-up.
    - **Connect notifications** to get form submission alerts in Slack or Teams.
    
    <Tip>All form submissions are automatically processed and stored securely!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
