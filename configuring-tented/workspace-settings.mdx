## Workspace Settings Overview

Workspace settings control how your team collaborates, organizes content, and manages projects within Tented. These settings affect all team members and determine the overall structure and behavior of your workspace.

## Team Management

### Team Information

**Basic Team Details:**
- **Team Name**: Display name for your team
- **Team Description**: Brief description of team purpose
- **Team Logo**: Upload team logo or avatar
- **Team Website**: Link to team or company website

**Team Settings:**
- **Team Type**: Business, agency, or personal
- **Industry**: Your business sector
- **Team Size**: Number of team members
- **Creation Date**: When the team was created

### Member Management

**Team Members:**
- **Active Members**: Current team members
- **Pending Invitations**: Invitations waiting for response
- **Member Roles**: Admin, editor, viewer, custom
- **Member Permissions**: What each member can do

**Role Definitions:**
- **Admin**: Full access to all features and settings
- **Editor**: Can create, edit, and publish tents
- **Viewer**: Can view tents and data, limited editing
- **Custom**: Specific permission sets as needed

**Invitation Management:**
- **Send Invitations**: Invite new team members
- **Invitation Templates**: Pre-configured invitation messages
- **Invitation Expiry**: How long invitations remain valid
- **Auto-approval**: Automatically approve certain invitations

## Workspace Organization

### Folder Structure

**Default Folders:**
- **Active Campaigns**: Currently running campaigns
- **Drafts**: Tents in development
- **Published**: Live tents
- **Archived**: Completed or retired tents

**Custom Folders:**
- **Create Folders**: Add custom organization folders
- **Folder Permissions**: Control folder access
- **Nested Folders**: Create subfolder structures
- **Folder Templates**: Pre-configured folder sets

**Folder Management:**
- **Move Tents**: Organize tents into folders
- **Bulk Operations**: Move multiple tents at once
- **Folder Sharing**: Share folders with specific members
- **Folder Analytics**: Track folder performance

### Tagging System

**Default Tags:**
- **Campaign Type**: Lead gen, product launch, event
- **Status**: Draft, published, archived
- **Priority**: High, medium, low
- **Client**: Client or project tags

**Custom Tags:**
- **Create Tags**: Add custom tags for your needs
- **Tag Colors**: Assign colors to tags for visual organization
- **Tag Categories**: Group related tags together
- **Tag Permissions**: Control who can create/edit tags

**Tag Management:**
- **Bulk Tagging**: Apply tags to multiple tents
- **Tag Filtering**: Filter tents by tags
- **Tag Analytics**: Track tag usage and performance
- **Tag Cleanup**: Remove unused or duplicate tags
