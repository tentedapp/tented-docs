## Publishing Overview

Publishing your tent makes it live on the web with a unique URL that you can share with your audience. The publishing process is simple and fast, taking just a few clicks to go from draft to live website.

## Publishing Process

### Initial Publishing

**Step 1: Review Your Tent**
- Ensure all content is accurate and complete
- Test all functionality in preview mode
- Verify mobile responsiveness
- Check form submissions work correctly

**Step 2: Click Publish**
- Navigate to your tent editor
- Click the **"Publish"** button
- Confirm publishing in the dialog
- Wait for the publishing process to complete

**Step 3: Get Your Live URL**
- Receive your unique tent URL
- Copy the URL for sharing
- Test the live site to ensure everything works
- Share with your audience

## Publishing Updates

### Updating Published Tents

**Making Changes:**
1. **Edit your tent** using the chat interface
2. **Preview changes** to ensure they look correct
3. **Click "Publish Update"** to push changes live
4. **Changes go live immediately** - no downtime

**What Gets Updated:**
- All content changes
- Design modifications
- New features or sections
- Bug fixes and improvements

**What Stays the Same:**
- Your tent URL remains unchanged
- Form submission data is preserved
- Analytics data continues tracking
- SEO rankings are maintained

### Version Control

**Publishing History:**
- Track all published versions
- See what changed in each update
- Revert to previous published versions
- Compare different published states

**Rollback Capability:**
- Quickly revert to previous version
- Emergency rollback for critical issues
- A/B testing different versions
- Gradual rollout of changes

## Domain Management

### Custom Domains (DNS Redirects)

**Setting Up Custom Domain:**
1. **Choose your domain** (e.g., `landing.yourcompany.com`)
2. **Add DNS record** pointing to tented.ai
3. **Configure redirect** in Tented settings
4. **Test the redirect** to ensure it works
