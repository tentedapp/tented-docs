## Tent Details Overview

The tent details page provides a comprehensive view of your tent's information, status, and management options. This is your central hub for understanding and controlling your tent.

## Page Layout

### Header Section

The header displays essential tent information:

- **Tent Name**: Click to edit/rename your tent
- **Status Badge**: Shows current status (Draft, Published, Generating, etc.)
- **Last Updated**: Timestamp of the most recent changes
- **Created By**: User who originally created the tent

## Generation Management

### Viewing Generations

Each tent maintains a history of all generations:

- **Generation Number**: Sequential version number
- **Created Date**: When this generation was made
- **Created By**: User who triggered the generation
- **Type**: Initial, Iteration, or Reverted
- **Status**: Completed, Failed, or In Progress

### Reverting Generations

To revert to a previous generation:

1. **Click on a previous generation** in the history
2. **Review the changes** that will be made
3. **Confirm the revert** action
4. **New generation created** with the reverted content

<Warning>
Reverting creates a new generation and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to lose recent changes before reverting.
</Warning>

## Quick Actions Menu

Access common actions from the three-dot menu:

- **Rename Tent**: Change the tent name
- **Duplicate**: Create a copy of the tent
- **Archive**: Move tent to archived status
- **Delete**: Permanently remove the tent
- **Export Code**: Download the HTML/CSS/JS files
- **Share Preview**: Get a shareable preview link

## Mobile Responsiveness

The tent details page is fully responsive:

- **Mobile View**: Optimized layout for phones and tablets
- **Touch Actions**: Swipe and tap gestures supported
- **Quick Access**: Essential actions easily accessible on mobile
- **Preview**: Mobile preview of your tent included
